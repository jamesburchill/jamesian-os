## Status Values

- Open
- In progress
- Fixed
- Verified

- Needs expert review

## Risk Categories

- Cosmetic

- Security Risk
- Data Integrity Risk
- Privacy Risk
- Financial Risk
- Legal/Compliance Risk
- Operational Risk
- Catastrophic Failure Risk

## Severity Values

- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

## Verification Notes

For each fixed issue, record:

- what changed
- who verified it
- when it was verified
- what evidence proves it is fixed
- what risk remains
